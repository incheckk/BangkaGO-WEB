Who We Are

Bridging Islands.
Connecting Lives.

BangkaGo is a pioneering island-based logistics and tourism platform built for the communities of Bangka Island and surrounding archipelagos in the Philippines. We combine the reliability of modern delivery services with the adventure of island exploration.

Our fleet of certified boats and trusted local riders ensures your parcels, groceries, and meals reach you safely — whether you're on the main island or a remote barangay across the sea.
